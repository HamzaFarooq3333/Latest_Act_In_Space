Orchestrating the orbital ballet.

When space becomes traffic, a single control tower can’t scale. Orbital Cells is a decentralized coordination layer where spacecraft negotiate locally in dynamic traffic cells, follow explainable safety rules, and learn efficiency policies in simulation.

Dual-use wedge

The same “traffic cell” coordination engine can ship on Earth first (revenue), then harden for space.

Drone corridors

Decentralized local rules for dense urban air mobility and inspection fleets.

Ports & logistics hubs

Coordinate autonomous ground vehicles safely to increase throughput and reduce downtime.

Mining & industrial sites

Operate in partially connected environments with robust fallback modes.

Rollout roadmap

A credible path from demo to deployment and qualification.

  1. Phase 1
    Earth pilots: deploy the coordination engine for fleets and hubs.
  2. Phase 2
    Space sandbox: simulation + policy evaluation for operators and agencies.
  3. Phase 3
    Space qualification: certified safety layer + flight demo on controlled missions.
